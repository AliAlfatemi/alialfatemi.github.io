Teaching

CISC 1100 R03: Structures of Computer Science

Fall 2025

Fordham University (Rose Hill Campus)

Mon & Thu, 5:30–6:45 PM
JMH 330
OH: Mon & Thu, 4:30–5:30 PM

This is an introductory course in the discrete structures used in computer and information technology. Emphasis is placed on the ability to solve problems and develop logical thinking.

Topics: Sets, Functions, Elementary Combinatorics, Discrete Probability, Logic, Boolean Algebra, Recursion, and Graphs.
